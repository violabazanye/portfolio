Hello Chef Bot!

Ever looked inside your fridge and found a bunch of random ingredients, but had no idea what to cook with them? I have..., plenty of times.
This gave me the idea to create your everday recipe app but with a twist. The twist being - with a conversational UI.

Objectives:

  • provide user with meaningful recipe suggestions
  • raise user curiosity and liking
  • display my interaction design and app development skills (using React Native)

Customized suggestions.

Start a conversation with Chef Bot to obtain customized recipes according to what is available in your fridge. Chef Bot is also adaptive - meaning that, depending on the situation, it can be modified and adjusted to achieve better results.
Here the artificial inttelligence will be powered by Facebook's Wit.ai.

Search for your favorite recipes!

Chef Bot will be powered by a popular open source API, Edamam, which gives it access to over 1.5 million recipes that you can choose from.

Your very own mobile sous-chef.
